### The Gypsy King's Return
Tyson Fury, the former heavyweight champion of the world, is gearing up for his comeback fight. According to his promoter, Frank Warren, Fury has his sights set on a big-name opponent after his return to the ring. None other than Oleksandr Usyk, the current unified cruiserweight champion, is the target.

### A Rivalry Renewed
Fury and Usyk have a history, having been involved in a heated rivalry in the past. The two fighters have exchanged words on numerous occasions, and a potential fight between them has been a topic of discussion among boxing fans for years. Now, it seems that Fury is eager to settle the score and prove his superiority over the Ukrainian fighter.

### The Road to Redemption
Fury's comeback fight is highly anticipated, and a win against a top-level opponent would be a significant step towards redeeming his reputation as one of the best heavyweight fighters in the world. Usyk, on the other hand, has been making waves in the boxing world with his impressive performances, and a fight against Fury would be a massive test for him.
