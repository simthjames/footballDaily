## 1. Commitment to Accuracy
Trust is the currency of journalism. At football Daily, we employ a rigorous fact-checking process. Every article is reviewed for statistical accuracy and factual correctness. If an error occurs, we are committed to correcting it promptly and transparently with a standardized "Correction Note" at the bottom of the article.

## 2. Sourcing & Attribution
We believe in giving credit where it is due.
*   **Official Sources:** We prioritize data from official league bodies, clubs, and reputable data providers (Opta/StatsPerform).
*   **Transfer News:** We distinguish between "Official Confirmations," "Reports from Credible Outlets," and "Rumors." We do not publish unsubstantiated clickbait.

## 3. Use of Technology & AI
football Daily utilizes advanced technology to assist in data aggregation, trend monitoring, and structuring historical data. However, **human oversight is mandatory**.
*   AI tools are used as *assistants* for research and structure.
*   All opinions, tactical breakdowns, and final editorial approvals are conducted by human analysts to ensure nuance, context, and emotional intelligence.

## 4. Conflict of Interest
Our writers and analysts are required to disclose any potential conflicts of interest. football Daily maintains strict independence from any football club, agency, or betting organization.
